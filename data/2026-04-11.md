### [1] [Optimization of 32-bit Unsigned Division by Constants on 64-bit Targets](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07902)
*Shigeo Mitsunari,Takashi Hoshino*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 该论文针对64位CPU优化了32位无符号整数除以常数的算法，相比现有方法（GM方法）能更好地利用64位能力，在Intel和Apple芯片上分别获得1.67x和1.98x的加速。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的无符号整数除以常数的优化方法（GM方法）主要针对32位CPU设计，在64位CPU上无法充分利用硬件能力。例如，对于x/7这样的运算，生成的代码仍然是32位优化的，存在性能提升空间。

Method: 提出了一种针对64位CPU优化的32位无符号整数除以常数的方法。作者为LLVM和GCC编译器实现了补丁，专门优化在64位架构上的除法运算。

Result: 在Intel Xeon w9-3495X（Sapphire Rapids）上获得1.67倍加速，在Apple M4芯片上获得1.98倍加速。LLVM补丁已经合并到llvm:main分支，证明了方法的实用性。

Conclusion: 该方法成功优化了64位CPU上的32位无符号整数除以常数运算，显著提升了性能，并已在实际编译器中得到应用，具有重要的工程价值。

Abstract: Granlund and Montgomery proposed an optimization method for unsigned integer division by constants [3]. Their method (called the GM method in this paper) was further improved in part by works such as [1] and [7], and is now adopted by major compilers including GCC, Clang, Microsoft Compiler, and Apple Clang. However, for example, for x/7, the generated code is designed for 32-bit CPUs and therefore does not fully exploit 64-bit capabilities. This paper proposes an optimization method for 32-bit unsigned division by constants targeting 64-bit CPUs. We implemented patches for LLVM/GCC and achieved speedups of 1.67x on Intel Xeon w9-3495X (Sapphire Rapids) and 1.98x on Apple M4 (Apple M-series SoC) in the microbenchmark described later. The LLVM patch has already been merged into llvm:main [6], demonstrating the practical applicability of the proposed method.

### [2] [PG-MDP: Profile-Guided Memory Dependence Prediction for Area-Constrained Cores](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08445)
*Luke Panayi,Johan Jino,Sebastian S. Kim,Alberto Ros,Alexandra Jimborean,Jim Whittaker,Martin Berger,Paul Kelly*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: PG-MDP通过软件协同设计，使用配置文件引导的内存依赖预测，标记持续内存独立的负载，使其绕过MDP查询，从而在小面积核心上实现接近大型预测器的性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 面积受限的核心（如能效或边缘系统）中，内存依赖预测表空间有限，导致高假依赖率，使内存独立负载与无关预测条目产生别名，造成处理器流水线不必要停顿。传统解决方案（增大预测器规模或复杂度）对面积受限核心不适用。

Method: 提出配置文件引导的内存依赖预测（PG-MDP），通过软件协同设计，使用配置文件标记持续内存独立的负载（通过操作码标识），将其从MDP工作集中移除。这些负载在调度时绕过MDP查询，始终尽快执行。

Result: 在SPEC2017 CPU intspeed测试中，PG-MDP将MDP查询率降低79%，假依赖减少77%，小规模模拟核心的几何平均IPC提升1.47%（接近使用16倍预测器条目的性能，差距仅0.5%），无面积成本，无额外指令带宽开销。

Conclusion: 针对预测器工作集进行优化与增大预测器规模同样有效，PG-MDP能在使用极小预测器的同时实现与大型预测器竞争的性能，为面积受限核心提供高效的内存依赖预测解决方案。

Abstract: Memory Dependence Prediction (MDP) is a speculative technique to determine which stores, if any, a given load will depend on. Area-constrained cores are increasingly relevant in various applications such as energy-efficient or edge systems, and often have limited space for MDP tables. This leads to a high rate of false dependencies as memory independent loads alias with unrelated predictor entries, causing unnecessary stalls in the processor pipeline.
  The conventional way to address this problem is with greater predictor size or complexity, but this is unattractive on area-constrained cores. This paper proposes that targeting the predictor working set is as effective as growing the predictor, and can deliver performance competitive with large predictors while still using very small predictors. This paper introduces profile-guided memory dependence prediction (PG-MDP), a software co-design to label consistently memory independent loads via their opcode and remove them from the MDP working set. These loads bypass querying the MDP when dispatched and always issue as soon as possible. Across SPEC2017 CPU intspeed, PG-MDP reduces the rate of MDP queries by 79%, false dependencies by 77%, and improves geomean IPC for a small simulated core by 1.47% (to within 0.5% of using 16x the predictor entries), with no area cost and no additional instruction bandwidth.

</details>

### [3] [When Equality Fails as a Rewrite Principle: Provenance and Definedness for Measurement-Bearing Expressions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07626)
*David B. Hulak,Arthur F. Ramos,Ruy J. G. B. de Queiroz*

Main category: cs.LO

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种跟踪溯源和定义性的统一语义，用于处理带有测量的表达式，解决了代数等式在测量表达式中的不健全问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 普通代数等式对于带有测量的表达式不是健全的重写原则，因为相同观测的重用会影响结果，除法操作会使代数相等的形式在定义域上产生差异。

Method: 提出了令牌敏感的包含语义，生成单向重写和可互换性判断；通过可容许域细化获得域安全的重写判断；支持相对变体连接局部和全局可容许性。

Result: 还原定理在普遍可容许的支持上恢复了基于包含的理论；恢复定理内化了消去、背景减法和正区间自除法；严格性定理显示可达奇点使简化成为单向操作。

Conclusion: 令牌身份对于区分代数相等但定义域不同的表达式至关重要，所有定义和定理都在Lean 4中形式化验证，无需"抱歉"假设。

Abstract: Ordinary algebraic equality is not a sound rewrite principle for measurement-bearing expressions. Reuse of the same observation matters, and division can make algebraically equal forms differ on where they are defined. We present a unified semantics that tracks both provenance and definedness. Token-sensitive enclosure semantics yields judgments for one-way rewriting and interchangeability. An admissible-domain refinement yields a domain-safe rewrite judgment, and support-relative variants connect local and global admissibility. Reduction theorems recover the enclosure-based theory on universally admissible supports. Recovery theorems internalize cancellation, background subtraction, and positive-interval self-division. Strictness theorems show that reachable singularities make simplification one-way and make common-domain equality too weak for licensed replacement. An insufficiency theorem shows that erasing token identity collapses distinctions that definedness alone cannot recover. All definitions and theorems are formalized in sorry-free Lean 4.

</details>
