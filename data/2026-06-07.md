### [1] [Incremental Computation for Efficient Programmable Inference in Probabilistic Programs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05348)
*Fabian Zaiser,Jack Czenszak,Martin C. Rinard,Vikash K. Mansinghka,Alexander K. Lew*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 本文提出将概率程序编译为确定性密度函数，并利用增量计算技术（基于增量λ演算）实现概率推理中中间结果的共享与重用，从而提升蒙特卡洛推理算法在大数据集上的效率，并在Julia原型中验证了渐近性能改进。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 概率程序推理需要评估大量执行路径，在大数据集上重复计算昂贵中间结果导致效率低下。现有系统采用特设的增量方法，不仅不够通用（难以支持非参数模型），而且容易引入正确性问题。

Method: 1. 将表达性概率程序编译为计算其密度函数的确定性程序；2. 基于增量λ演算，提出一种通用的函数式程序增量计算方法，并应用于密度函数；3. 将过程解耦为密度计算和增量变换两个独立步骤，分别通过指称逻辑关系论证正确性。

Result: 在Julia原型上实现，实验表明该方法在一系列模型和推理算法上，随着数据集规模的增大，实现了渐近运行时间改进。

Conclusion: 增量计算可以有效加速概率程序的蒙特卡洛推理，且通过将密度计算与增量化解耦，可以模块化地保证正确性，避免了现有系统中因特设增量而导致的正确性漏洞。

Abstract: Inference in probabilistic programs generally requires evaluating many possible program executions to find those of high posterior density. To scale inference to large datasets, it is crucial that expensive intermediate results are shared across these many evaluations, rather than recomputed from scratch. This paper presents a new approach to realizing this sharing, based on \textit{incremental computation}, a technique for efficiently recomputing (deterministic) program outputs when program inputs change. First, we show how expressive probabilistic programs can be compiled to deterministic ones that compute their density functions. Then, building on the incremental $λ$-calculus, we develop a general technique for compositionally incrementalizing expressive functional programs, and apply it to these densities. The resulting incremental densities can be used to accelerate a broad range of Monte Carlo inference algorithms, including for nonparametric models not well supported by existing systems. Furthermore, our decomposition of incremental density computation into separate density and incrementalization steps allows for modular reasoning about correctness -- a key pain point in existing systems, where ad-hoc incrementalization features are a known source of soundness bugs. We develop denotational logical relations arguments for the correctness of each step independently, and implement the approach in a Julia prototype, finding that it leads to asymptotic runtime improvements in the size of the dataset on a range of models and inference algorithms.

### [2] [Look Before You Leap: Checking in on Type Tag Checking](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05466)
*Stephen M. Watt*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 本文重新评估了动态语言系统中标签化通用值的性能权衡，通过在AArch64和x86-64平台上比较badged对象头、低位标签和NaN-boxing三种方案，发现低位标签在符号型负载中简单高效，而NaN-boxing在浮点值场景中避免堆分配成本。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着机器架构和工作负载的演变，旧的关于装箱值、立即值和类型标签的经验法则需要重新校准，因此需要重新评估不同标签化方案的性能权衡。

Method: 在多个制造商的AArch64和x86-64平台上，通过实验隔离两种效应（避免堆分配标量值的成本、从值字而非堆读取获取标签信息的成本），比较badged对象头、低位标签和两种NaN-boxing布局的性能。

Result: 局部位操作通常比打开堆对象获取标签或小值更便宜；低位标签在符号型工作负载中是最简单且最快的选择；NaN-boxing在访问成本上接近低位标签，且能避免普通浮点值的堆分配开销（时间和空间）。

Conclusion: 不同标签化方案在不同场景下各有优劣，低位标签适合符号型负载，NaN-boxing适合浮点密集型负载，方案选择需根据具体工作负载特性决定。

Abstract: Tagging of generic dynamic values is important in symbolic-computation and dynamic-language systems, but the trade-offs change as machine architectures and workloads evolve. In particular, old folklore about boxed values, immediate values, and type tags must be recalibrated from time to time. We revisit the performance of badged object headers, low-bit tagging, and two NaN-boxing layouts on a range of platforms in use today, including AArch64 and x86-64 architectures from different manufacturers. The experiments isolate two distinct effects: the cost avoided by not heap-allocating common scalar values, and the cost avoided by obtaining tag information from the value word rather than by performing a heap read. The results show that several local bit operations are often cheaper than opening a heap object to obtain a tag or small value. Low-bit tagging remains the simplest and usually fastest choice for mostly symbolic workloads, while NaN-boxing is close in access cost and avoids the time and space of heap allocation for ordinary floating-point values.

### [3] [CASS-RTL: Correctness-Aware Subspace Steering for RTL Generation with LLMs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05680)
*Mohammad Akyash,Nowfel Mashnoor,Kimia Azar,Hadi Kamali*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 本文提出CASS-RTL框架，通过识别大语言模型中与RTL正确性相关的注意力头，构建低维子空间并进行轻量级推理干预，将RTL代码生成准确率提升10%-20%，无需额外监督或重训练。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有LLM生成RTL代码的方法多依赖外部验证、微调等策略，忽略了模型内部注意力机制与RTL正确性的内在关联，缺乏从模型内部出发提升生成准确性的方法。

Method: (i) 识别注意力头中能区分正确/错误RTL的激活模式；(ii) 构建捕获正确性相关信号的低维子空间；(iii) 设计轻量级几何感知干预，在推理时引导模型输出。

Result: 在VerilogEval上pass@1/5/10提升10%-20%，在CVDP上提升5%，且方法无需额外监督或重训练，模型无关。

Conclusion: CASS-RTL通过利用LLM内部正确性感知组件，有效提升了RTL代码生成的可靠性和准确性，为芯片设计自动化提供了新思路。

Abstract: Recent advances in large language models (LLMs) have enabled the automatic synthesis (generation) of register-transfer level (RTL) code from natural language instructions, offering a promising pathway to accelerate chip design. Unlike typical natural language (and software coding) tasks, LLM-based RTL code generation demands strict cycle accuracy with concurrency, where minor logical errors can render a circuit unusable or insecure. While prior work has explored hallucination mitigation via external verification, self-evaluation prompts, retrieval-augmented prompting, domain specific fine-tuning, agentic solutions, and reasoning, these approaches largely overlook the attention-oriented internal mechanisms of LLMs that may inherently correlate with RTL correctness. This work proposes CASS-RTL, a first-of-its-kind framework for discovering and leveraging LLMs' correctness-aware components to guide RTL generation toward functionally accurate outputs. We (i) identify attention heads whose activation patterns consistently differentiate correct from incorrect RTL; (ii) construct a low-dimensional subspace capturing correctness-relevant signals; and (iii) design a lightweight, geometry-aware intervention that steers the model at inference time. CASS-RTL is fully model-agnostic, requires no additional supervision or retraining, and readily integrates into existing models. Empirically, we evaluate CASS-RTL on multiple models and observe 10%-20% improvement in pass@1/5/10 accuracy on VerilogEval and 5% improvement on CVDP, demonstrating the effectiveness of our method in enhancing reliability without sacrificing model efficiency or requiring a large labeled dataset for fine-tuning.

### [4] [Where Do Large Language Models Fail on Competitive Programming? A Taxonomy of Failures by Algorithm Type and Difficulty Rating](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05228)
*Ayush Kumar Jha,Shalini Jha*

Main category: cs.SE

TL;DR: 本文通过315道Codeforces题目的系统实验发现，强制使用Chain-of-Thought推理会显著降低GPT-4o的通过率（46.0%→36.8%），并导致Claude的编译错误增加244%，揭示了标准提示工程技术在算法推理场景中的失效。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有LLM报告仅发布聚合通过率，掩盖了模型在特定算法领域和错误类型上的脆弱性，缺乏细粒度的失败模式分析。

Method: 构建包含7种算法类别和3个难度等级的315道Codeforces平衡分类题库；在严格执行条件下（温度T=0.2）评估GPT-4o和Claude Sonnet 4.6；通过消融实验对比直接零样本生成与零样本Chain-of-Thought推理的效果。

Result: CoT强制推理使GPT-4o通过率从46.0%降至36.8%，并加剧其在Greedy逻辑上的缺陷；Claude在CoT下虽保持较高逻辑基线（63.5%），但编译错误从9增至31（+244%）；未通过解法中，Wrong Answer占GPT-4o的90%以上、Claude的约70%。

Conclusion: 标准提示工程技术无法弥合竞争性编程环境中的算法推理差距，CoT对不同模型的影响存在显著且非对称的负面效应。

Abstract: Large language models (LLMs) demonstrate increasing proficiency on competitive programming benchmarks, yet technical reports predominantly publish aggregate pass rates, obscuring domain-specific vulnerabilities. We present a systematic empirical study of LLM failure patterns using a balanced taxonomy of 315 Codeforces problems across seven algorithm categories and three difficulty tiers. We evaluate GPT-4o and Claude Sonnet 4.6 under strict execution-based conditions, controlling for temperature (T = 0.2). To isolate the impact of reasoning frameworks on algorithmic correctness, we conduct an ablation study comparing direct zero-shot generation against zero-shot Chain-of-Thought (CoT). Our findings reveal a severe divergence from standard NLP benchmarks: forcing CoT aggressively penalizes GPT-4o, dropping its pass rate from 46.0% to 36.8% and exacerbating a critical weakness in Greedy logic. Conversely, while Claude maintains a higher logical baseline (63.5% under CoT), the expanded text generation severely degrades its markdown instruction adherence, causing its Compile Errors to more than triple (from 9 to 31, a 244% increase). Furthermore, failure-mode analysis indicates that Wrong Answer (WA) is the dominant verdict for both models--accounting for over 90% of GPT-4o's and roughly 70% of Claude's unaccepted solutions. These findings empirically demonstrate that standard prompt engineering techniques fail to bridge the algorithmic reasoning gap in competitive programming environments.

</details>
