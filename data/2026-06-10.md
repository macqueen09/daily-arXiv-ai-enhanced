### [1] [Compile Once, Differentiate Everywhere: A Differentiable Meta-Circular Interpreter](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09930)
*Lucas Sheneman*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 本文提出一个编译器，将Scheme子集翻译为可微计算图，实现可微元循环解释（DMCI）。DMCI让编译后的Scheme解释器对程序数据自动求导，无需重新编译。在电池退化与厄尔尼诺反问题中，DMCI结合大语言模型实现程序结构与连续参数的联合搜索，优于无梯度方法和手工基线。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 程序执行与梯度优化的边界长期存在，限制了将代码本身作为可学习科学模型的使用。现有方法要么依赖封闭表达式，要么需要为每个程序定制梯度机制，缺乏通用性。

Method: 设计一个编译器，将自托管Scheme子集翻译为可微计算图（适配自动微分后端）。由于该子集能编译自身的求值器，实现了可微元循环解释（DMCI）：编译后的Scheme解释器将程序作为数据处理，反向模式自动微分将梯度传播到嵌入程序的连续常数。解释器只编译一次，新程序无需重新编译即可继承可微性，保留闭包、递归和数据结构。

Result: 定理证明梯度正确性几乎处处成立；数值实验显示与直接编译精度相当（171个递归和高阶程序-种子对）。在电池容量衰减数据上，搜索恢复膝形退化结构，在早期外推分集中改进手工基线；在高维El Nino反问题中，DMCI优化了解释型卡尔曼滤波似然，而无梯度搜索失败。

Conclusion: 将符号回归和神经符号搜索从封闭表达式扩展到可执行的、有状态的程序，使模型生成的代码可以直接针对数据优化。DMCI为程序结构与连续参数的联合搜索提供了通用框架。

Abstract: The boundary between program execution and gradient-based optimization has long limited the use of code itself as a learnable scientific model. We present a compiler that translates a self-hosting subset of Scheme into differentiable computation graphs for autograd backends. Because the subset can compile its own evaluator, this yields differentiable meta-circular interpretation (DMCI): a compiled Scheme interpreter executes programs supplied as data, while reverse-mode autodiff propagates gradients to continuous constants embedded in those programs. The interpreter is compiled once, so new programs inherit differentiability without recompilation or custom gradient machinery, while retaining closures, recursion, and data structures. We prove that gradients through the compiled interpreter are correct almost everywhere and show that they match direct compilation to numerical precision across 171 recursive and higher-order program-seed pairs. We then use DMCI for program-and-parameter co-search, where a large language model proposes Scheme programs and exact gradients calibrate their continuous parameters through a single frozen interpreter. This enables OpenEvolve-style program search in which an outer loop proposes discrete program structures and DMCI supplies exact gradient-based calibration of each candidate's continuous parameters. On battery capacity-fade data, the search recovers a knee-like degradation structure and improves held-out extrapolation over hand-crafted baselines on the harder early-extrapolation split, matching them on the later split. On a high-dimensional El Nino inverse problem, DMCI optimizes an interpreted Kalman-filter likelihood where gradient-free search fails. These results extend symbolic regression and neurosymbolic search from closed-form expressions to executable, stateful programs, making model-generated code directly optimizable against data.

### [2] [Answer Set Programming for Egg Extraction and More](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10644)
*Ziyi Yang,Ilya Sergey*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 本文探讨了如何使用回答集编程（ASP）改进e-graph中的项提取任务，发现简单的ASP编码在效率上与优化的ILP方法相当，并能找到更多最优解，进而提出了ASP作为更强大Datalog的"更好结合"可能性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 尽管e-graph项提取是NP-hard问题，且ASP能自然建模该任务，但三年前的初步尝试效果不佳。作者希望从实践角度证明ASP可以有效解决这一问题。

Method: 通过实际的ASP使用视角，设计简单编码方案，与现有的基于ILP的精确DAG提取方法在extraction-gym基准上进行效率对比。

Result: 简单的ASP编码在效率上与经过良好优化的ILP方法相当，并且在复杂实例上发现了额外的最优提取方案。

Conclusion: ASP作为比Datalog更强大的工具，与e-graph系统结合存在相互受益的潜力，值得进一步探索"更好结合"的方式。

Abstract: Three years ago, Philip Zucker posted an attempt to use answer set programming (ASP) for term extraction from e-graphs Although the task is NP-hard and ASP offers a natural modelling of e-graph terms, the initial attempt did not yield convincing results.
  From the aspect of practical ASP users, we first pinpoint the way to make ASP work and work well on the task of e-graph extraction. The initial results show the naïve ASP encoding is comparable on efficiency to the well-optimised ILP-based exact DAG extraction in the extraction-gym, and find several extra optimal extraction on the complex instances. This leads us to a further agenda: with the "better together of egg+Datalog", is there a better "better together" by having ASP as a more powerful Datalog? We discuss the potential benefit from each other.

### [3] [Max-Policy Iteration, Revisited](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10717)
*David Monniaux,Helmut Seidl*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 本文提出用值迭代替代数学优化来执行最大策略迭代，从而高效计算数值程序不变量，并扩展到最大-最小策略迭代以处理有理数系统。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 数学优化计算成本高，传统最大策略迭代因依赖优化而效率受限，需要更高效的替代方案。

Method: 用值迭代替代数学优化；提出最小策略迭代作为线性规划的替代；将算法从有界系统扩展到无界系统；构建正确性与最优性证书。

Result: 值迭代替代优化后仍保证终止；最大-最小策略迭代保证返回有界系统的最小解；可扩展到无界系统并生成证书。

Conclusion: 所提方法避免了宽度算子，实现了整数/浮点变量的精确界分析，且在有界系统上保证最优解。

Abstract: Max-policy iteration is an approach to computing precise numeric program invariants by successive attempts at resolving maximum operators and reduction to mathematical optimization. Mathematical optimization, though, may be expensive. Here, we show, for max-policy iteration on systems of equations over integers as well as over floating point numbers, that mathematical optimization can be replaced by plain value iteration -- which is still guaranteed to terminate. As an application, a precise bound analysis for integer or floating point variables is obtained, avoiding widening operators altogether. We also consider max-policy iteration over the rational numbers, where the right-hand sides are maxima of minima of affine combinations of unknowns. We propose min-policy iteration as an alternative to linear programming for solving the optimization problems posed by max-policy iteration. We prove that max-min policy iteration is guaranteed to return the least solution for bounded systems. We also show how to extend this algorithm to unbounded systems, and how to construct certificates of soundness as well as of optimality of the computed results.

</details>
