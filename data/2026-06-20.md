### [1] [Effect Systems as Abstract Interpretations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19686)
*Colin S. Gordon*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 本文通过将效应量化子嵌入抽象域，并从事件发生角度将效应系统重构为抽象解释，建立了抽象解释与效应系统之间的形式化关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 尽管抽象解释能捕捉类型系统，但类型-效应系统与抽象解释之间的一般形式关系尚未明确，现有研究对此缺乏系统性的形式化建立。

Method: 首先将效应量化子嵌入抽象域；然后从事件发生（而非状态或值）的角度，将效应量化子的一般形式恢复为抽象解释。

Result: 成功建立了效应系统与抽象解释之间的形式化对应关系，证明了效应量化子可以视为一种基于事件发生的抽象解释。

Conclusion: 该工作为类型-效应系统与抽象解释之间提供了严格的形式化桥梁，有助于理解两种静态分析框架之间的深层联系。

Abstract: Many forms of static reasoning about program behaviours are known in the literature, yet formal relationships are studied surprisingly infrequently. While most type systems are well-known to be captured by abstract interpretations, the situation for type-and-effect systems is, in the general case, unsettled despite strong hypotheses and occasional framing of effect systems as abstract interpretations.
  We develop a formal relationship between abstract interpretations and a general class of effect systems. First, we describe an embedding of effect quantales into abstract domains. Second, we recover the general form of an effect quantale as an abstract interpretation -- not on states or values, but on event occurrences.

### [2] [How LLMs Fail and Generalize in RTL Coding for Hardware Design?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19347)
*Guan-Ting Liu,Chao-Han Huck Yang,Chenhui Deng,Zhongzhi Yu,Brucek Khailany,Yu-Chiang Frank Wang*

Main category: cs.CL

TL;DR: 本文研究了LLM在硬件设计（Verilog生成）中的瓶颈，提出了基于问题可解性的错误分类法，发现前沿模型在VerilogEval基准上存在90.8%通过率的上限，由不可解功能错误主导，且对齐技术仅能修复语法错误，无法突破预训练知识边界。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将顺序编程先验知识转化为硬件设计的并行时序逻辑仍是LLM的关键瓶颈，现有研究缺乏对错误类型的系统分类和理解，需要揭示模型在RTL编码中的根本限制。

Method: 提出基于问题可解性的错误分类法（受认知理论启发），将错误分为语法型、语义型、可解功能型和不可解功能型四类，在VerilogEval基准上评估前沿模型，分析表面收敛差距和测试时计算扩展的影响。

Result: 前沿模型在VerilogEval上通过率停滞在90.8%；不可解功能错误定义了性能上限；优化可消除语法错误但加剧深层功能失败；对齐技术仅教会模型编译；重复采样可修复可解错误；RTL编码能力严格受限于预训练知识。

Conclusion: 当前LLM驱动的硬件生成面临知识缺口这一根本限制，测试时计算扩展对此无效。未来应聚焦提升模型推理能力，而非对齐干预。

Abstract: Translating sequential programming priors into the parallel temporal logic of hardware design remains a crucial bottleneck for large language models(LLM). To investigate this, we introduce a new error taxonomy grounded in problem solvability, inspired by cognitive theory. Our taxonomy categorizes failures into syntactic, semantic, solvable functional, and unsolvable functional types. Evaluations reveal a strict empirical ceiling on the VerilogEval benchmark, as frontier models plateau at a 90.8% initial pass rate. These plateaus are defined by unsolvable functional errors, exposing persistent knowledge gaps immune to test time compute scaling. Furthermore, we expose a striking surface convergence gap: optimization readily eliminates syntax errors but concurrently exacerbates deeper functional failures. Our findings demonstrate that alignment techniques merely teach models to compile. While repeated sampling strategies can patch solvable errors, register-transfer level(RTL) coding capacity remains strictly bounded by pretraining knowledge. Addressing challenges in the current LLM based hardware generation pipeline requires more studies in model reasoning rather than alignment interventions.

### [3] [An MSO Framework for Weak-Memory Verification and Robustness](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20134)
*Giovanna Kobus Conrado,Andreas Pavlogiannis*

Main category: cs.LO

TL;DR: 本文研究Monadic Second-Order逻辑（MSO）作为弱内存模型的元理论，证明了不同内存模型在树宽和MSO可公理化方面的性质，并提出了reads-from鲁棒性概念，为弱内存验证建立了一个丰富的理论框架。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着弱内存模型数量和复杂性的增加，需要一种统一的元理论来支持跨模型的验证。本文旨在探索MSO逻辑是否能够作为这样一个统一的元理论框架。

Method: 通过证明各种弱内存模型（SC、TSO、Release/Acquire、RC20等）的树宽有界性及MSO可公理化性，并引入reads-from鲁棒性概念，构建统一的验证框架。

Result: (1) SC执行具有有界树宽，而TSO不具有；(2) Release/Acquire和RC20是MSO可公理化的，而Strong Release/Acquire和TSO不是（除非正交向量问题可在线性时间内解决）；(3) 对于MSO可公理化的模型，存在统一算法可验证程序或报告其非reads-from鲁棒。

Conclusion: 建立了一个丰富且多功能的弱内存验证与鲁棒性理论框架，为统一处理多种弱内存模型的验证问题提供了理论基础。

Abstract: Memory models are formal specifications of concurrent-program executions, accounting for weak behaviors introduced by compiler and architectural optimizations. The increase of their number and complexity has spawned efforts for uniform verification across whole classes of models, by axiomatizing the models in an adequate metatheory that admits a uniform treatment. In this work, we formally study Monadic Second-Order logic (MSO) as a metatheory for weak memory, by proving results on the treewidth and MSO-expressibility of various popular weak-memory models, as this combination allows us to uniformly tackle several verification problems. In summary, our results are as follows.
  First, we prove that executions under Sequential Consistency ($\mathsf{SC}$) have bounded treewidth, while already those under Total Store Order ($\mathsf{TSO}$) do not. Second, we prove that a broad range of models, including Release/Acquire and the full RC20, are MSO-axiomatizable, while others, such as Strong Release/Acquire and $\mathsf{TSO}$, are not, unless the Orthogonal Vectors problem $\unicode{x2013}$ which requires quadratic time under SETH $\unicode{x2013}$ can be solved in linear time. Finally, we introduce the notion of reads-from robustness, as an extension to recent work on coarse robustness criteria. We show that our treewidth bounds (both upper and lower) have far-reaching algorithmic implications for any of our MSO-axiomatizable models $\mathsf{MM}$: there is an algorithm that, for every program $\mathsf{P}$, either verifies $\mathsf{P}$ under $\mathsf{MM}$ or reports that $\mathsf{P}$ is not reads-from robust against $\mathsf{MM}$. Overall, our results establish a rich and versatile theoretical framework for weak-memory verification and robustness.

### [4] [A cubical formalisation of conditional independence, Bayesian conditioning, and Pearl's d-separation soundness](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20351)
*Karen Sargsyan*

Main category: cs.LO

TL;DR: 本文证明标准凸-代数交换公理不足以支持完全贝叶斯条件化，并在Cubical Agda中通过高阶归纳类型（HIT）构造有限分布，提出最小推广公理，验证了贝叶斯条件化、do-演算、d-分离等核心定理，最终将概率单子验证为马尔可夫范畴。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 标准概率单子中的凸-代数交换公理（convex-algebra interchange axiom）被证明过弱，无法支持完全的贝叶斯条件化（full Bayesian conditioning），因此需要更一般的代数结构来正确建模贝叶斯推理。

Method: 在Cubical Agda中，将有限分布建模为高阶归纳类型（HIT），条件独立性表示为核之间的立方路径（cubical path），递归贝叶斯条件化定义为全支撑片段上的全函数。通过分析HIT提升条件化时暴露的结构不匹配，提出最小推广公理，并证明标准公理是退化特例。

Result: 验证了bind交换律、四个半图洛伊公理、交性质（通过结构Σ见证缩减为收缩，无需正性条件）、Pearl do-演算规则1-3的核形式、有限类型贝叶斯条件化，以及任意有限有向无环图（DAG）上d-分离定理（干预与贝叶斯两种形式）的可靠性。概率单子被验证为马尔可夫范畴。

Conclusion: 标准交换公理不足以支持完全贝叶斯条件化，本文提出的最小推广公理解决了该问题，并在抽象有序域接口上构造性地验证了贝叶斯与因果推理所需的代数与图模型核心定理。

Abstract: The standard convex-algebra interchange axiom, common to probability-monad formalisations since Stone, is provably too weak to support full Bayesian conditioning. We make this precise in Cubical Agda: finite distributions as a higher inductive type, conditional independence as a cubical path between kernels, recursive Bayesian conditioning as a total function on a full-support fragment. Lifting conditioning to the full HIT exposes a structural mismatch -- the two halves of the rearranged 4-leaf mix carry distinct Bayesian weights related by Bayes' formula, not the single shared inner weight the standard axiom provides. We exhibit the minimal generalisation that resolves this and prove the standard form is the degenerate case where the two inner weights coincide. Around this observation we verify the algebraic context constructively, with zero postulates above an abstract ordered-field interface: bind commutativity, the four semi-graphoid axioms, intersection (reduced to contraction via structural $Σ$-witnesses, without positivity), Pearl's do-calculus Rules~1, 2, and~3 in kernel form, finite-type Bayesian conditioning, and Pearl's d-separation theorem (soundness) on arbitrary $n$-vertex finite directed acyclic graphs (DAGs) in both interventional and Bayesian forms. The probability monad is also verified as a Markov category; the abstract interface discharges at $Q$.

### [5] [OpenRath: Session-Centered Runtime State for Agent Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19409)
*Fukang Wen,Zhijie Wang,Ruilin Xu*

Main category: cs.SE

TL;DR: OpenRath提出了一种以Session为核心一等运行时抽象的多智能体编程模型，将碎片化的运行时状态统一封装为可分支、可检查、可重放、可组合的值，实现显式的运行时操作而非事后重建。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有智能体系统运行时状态碎片化严重：对话记录、工具效果、记忆事件、工作区放置、分支来源和重放证据各自分离存储，难以检查和复现。

Method: 提出类似PyTorch的编程模型（类比的是运行时抽象而非张量计算），核心抽象是Session——在智能体和工作流之间传递的运行时值。Session具有可分支、可检查、可重放、后端感知、可组合等特性，封装了对话片段、沙箱放置、谱系元数据、token使用、待处理工作和工具证据。同时定义了Sandbox、Tool、Agent、Memory、Workflow、Selector等组件，其中Selector将控制流转为运行时路由决策。

Result: 提出了编程模型、架构、审计里程碑和证据协议。声明限于受控运行时属性，定量比较、供应商质量、后端可用性和记忆质量留待后续评估。

Conclusion: 核心论点是Session为智能体系统提供了可审计组合的一等运行时值。

Abstract: Modern agent systems often suffer from fragmented runtime state: transcripts, tool effects, memory events, workspace placement, branch provenance, and replay evidence are recorded separately and become difficult to inspect or reproduce. OpenRath addresses this issue with a PyTorch-like programming model for multi-agent, multi-session systems. The analogy concerns the role of a central first-class runtime abstraction, not tensor computation. Its core abstraction is Session, the runtime value passed between agents and workflows. A Session is branchable, inspectable, replayable, backend-aware, and composable. It records conversation chunks, sandbox placement, lineage metadata, token usage, pending work, and tool evidence, while defining where memory interactions enter the runtime record. Since this state is carried by the same value used in program execution, fork, merge, and replay become explicit runtime operations rather than states reconstructed from external traces. OpenRath further defines Sandbox, Tool, Agent, Memory, Workflow, and Selector, with Selector turning control flow into runtime-routed decisions. This report presents the programming model, architecture, audited milestones, and evidence protocol. Its claims are limited to controlled runtime properties, while broad quantitative comparisons, live-provider quality, optional-backend availability, and memory quality are left for follow-on evaluation. The central thesis is that Session provides agent systems with a first-class runtime value for auditable composition.

### [6] [Multi-LCB: Extending LiveCodeBench to Multiple Programming Languages](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20517)

TL;DR: 本文提出 Multi-LCB，将 LiveCodeBench（仅限 Python）扩展为支持 12 种编程语言的多语言代码生成基准，揭示了 LLM 在 Python 过拟合、语言特异性污染及多语言性能差异等问题。

Abstract: LiveCodeBench (LCB) has recently become a widely adopted benchmark for evaluating large language models (LLMs) on code-generation tasks.   We evaluated 24 LLMs for instruction and reasoning on Multi-LCB, uncovering evidence of Python overfitting, language-specific contamination, and substantial disparities in multilingual performance. Our results establish Multi-LCB as a rigorous new benchmark for multi-programming-language code evaluation, directly addressing LCB's primary limitation and exposing critical gaps in current LLM capabilities.

### [7] [VERITAS: Verifier-Guided Proof Search for Zero-Shot Formal Theorem Proving](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19399)
*Manish Acharya,Zhenyu Liao,Yueke Zhang,Kevin Leach,Yu Huang,Yifan Zhang*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: VERITAS是一个零样本形式化证明框架，通过两阶段协议（Best-of-N采样 + 基于批评的MCTS）将验证器信号反馈给证明搜索，在miniF2F上达40.6%，并发布了VERITAS-CombiBench组合学基准。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的基于LLM的形式化证明器通常将丰富的验证器信号（如语法错误、类型不匹配、部分目标进展）简化为二元的通过/失败信息，丢失了大量有用的反馈信息。

Method: 提出VERITAS框架，采用两阶段协议：第一阶段进行Best-of-N采样，第二阶段使用批评引导的MCTS，将第一阶段的失败作为显式负样本输入，从而利用验证器的全部信号。

Result: 在miniF2F上达到40.6%（Best-of-5为36.9%，Portfolio为26.2%）；在VERITAS-CombiBench（55个定理的组合学基准）上达7.3%，而Best-of-5仅1.8%，Portfolio为3.6%。

Conclusion: 将丰富的验证器信号反馈回证明搜索能显著提升性能，尤其在需要从验证器反馈中迭代恢复正确引理名称的任务中，无引导的采样反而有害。

Abstract: LLM-based formal provers often collapse rich verifier signals (syntax errors, type mismatches, partial goal progress) into a binary pass/fail bit. We present VERITAS, a zero-shot framework that routes every verifier signal back into proof search through a two-phase protocol: Best-of-N sampling first, then a critic-guided MCTS pass that ingests Phase 1 failures as explicit negative examples. The protocol preserves every theorem solved by its own Phase 1 sweep, so Phase 2's additional solves are attributable to feedback-driven exploration. VERITAS reaches 40.6% on miniF2F (vs. an independently run Best-of-5 at 36.9%, Portfolio 26.2%) and 7.3% on VERITAS-CombiBench, a 55-theorem combinatorics benchmark we release on which Best-of-5 (1.8%) falls below Portfolio (3.6%), exposing that unguided sampling hurts when correct lemma names must be recovered iteratively from verifier feedback. Artifacts are available on GitHub.

</details>
