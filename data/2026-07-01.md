### [1] [NSynC: Normalised Synthesis of Computation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.30703)
*Zoey Shepherd,Ohad Kammar,Elizabeth Polgreen*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: NSynC是一种通过直接枚举语义而非语法来避免程序合成中语义重复的方法，在简单类型λ演算上实现了8.93倍的加速。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统基于语法的归纳式程序合成算法会生成大量语义相同但语法不同的程序，导致搜索空间庞大且合成效率低下，资源浪费严重。

Method: 提出NSynC方法，直接枚举目标语言的语义（正规形式），使用自顶向下、类型驱动的合成算法搜索简单类型λ演算（含和类型）的正规形式空间，确保每个候选程序语义唯一。

Result: 在合成基准测试集上，相比非限制算法，几何平均加速比达到8.93倍。

Conclusion: 通过语义枚举避免重复评估，显著提升程序合成效率。

Abstract: Inductive program synthesis algorithms search a space of programs to find one that meets some specification. Enumerating according to the syntax of a programming language leads to a large search space, and hence slow synthesis, due in large part to semantic duplication. A synthesiser may have to evaluate -- and reject -- multiple semantically identical but syntactically different programs, wasting resources.
  To avoid this duplication, we present NSynC, a synthesis-by-semantics approach. By enumerating the semantics of the target language directly, we guarantee that each candidate program is semantically unique and that each evaluation of a candidate is meaningful. Specifically, we search the space of normal forms for the simply-typed lambda calculus with sums using a top-down, type-directed synthesis algorithm. Our preliminary results show a geomean speedup of 8.93x on a synthetic benchmark suite over the unrestricted algorithm.

### [2] [When Do Staging Annotations Preserve Semantics? Mechanizing Typed Semantics-Preserving Multi-Stage Programming with Let-Insertion (Extended Version)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.30854)
*Jun Tan,Guannan Wei*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 本文研究如何设计保证语义保持的多阶段编程语言，提出两种带类型的两阶段演算 $λ_{|2|}$ 和 $λ^{ref}_{|2|}$（后者支持第二阶段的可变引用），通过自动 let-插入机制和轻量级类型-效果系统，实现类型安全且语义保持的代码片段操作，并利用二元逻辑关系证明了强语义保持定理。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有带引号的多阶段语言（如 MetaML、MetaOCaml、Template Haskell、Scala 3）虽然保证了生成代码的类型正确性，但语法级别的代码片段操作会微妙地改变求值顺序，导致阶段化程序与其未阶段化参考实现之间存在语义差异，这阻碍了阶段化代码作为未阶段化代码的"即插即用"替代品。

Method: 提出两种静态类型的两阶段演算：$λ_{|2|}$（基础版本）和 $λ^{ref}_{|2|}$（支持第二阶段可变引用）。动态语义建模自动 let-插入（作为控制效果在轻量级类型-效果系统中追踪），从而实现类型安全且语义保持的有效代码片段操作。使用二元逻辑关系证明强语义保持定理。

Result: 证明了强语义保持定理：如果一个良类型的二阶段程序 $t_1$ 求值为值 $\mathsf{code} t_2$，那么 $t_2$ 与 $t_1$ 的阶段擦除（stage-erasure）是上下文等价的。这为 Inoue 和 Taha 关于"何时阶段化注释保持语义"的问题提供了简洁且确定的答案。

Conclusion: 本文的演算及其机械化元理论为语义保持的多阶段编程奠定了坚实基础，解决了多阶段编程中长期存在的语义一致性问题，使阶段化代码可以作为未阶段化代码的可靠替代品。

Abstract: Multi-stage programming with quotations has long provided a powerful way to generate and manipulate code. By treating code as data, programmers can write multi-stage programs in which earlier stages produce specialized code from inputs available at generation time. Modern typed multi-stage languages (e.g., MetaML, MetaOCaml, Template Haskell, and Scala 3) adopt quotation/splicing constructs while enforcing the well-typedness of generated code. However, manipulating code fragments syntactically can subtly change evaluation order, leading to semantic discrepancies between a staged program and its unstaged counterpart, which is intended to serve as a reference implementation in many cases. The inconsistency complicates reasoning about correctness, and prevents staged code from being a drop-in replacement for its unstaged counterpart.
  In this paper, we study the design of multi-stage languages with semantics preservation guarantees. We develop two statically typed two-stage calculi, $λ_{|2|}$ and $λ^{ref}_{|2|}$, the latter supporting mutable references in the second stage. Their dynamic semantics models automatic let-insertion, tracked as a control effect in a lightweight type-and-effect system, enabling type-safe and semantics-preserving manipulation of effectful code fragments. We develop binary logical relations to prove strong semantics-preservation theorems: if a well-typed two-stage program $t_1$ evaluates to a value $\mathsf{code} t_2$, then $t_2$ is contextually equivalent to the stage-erasure of $t_1$. Our calculi and their mechanized metatheory provide a simple and definitive answer to the question posed by Inoue and Taha of when staging annotations preserve semantics, and lay a foundation for future work on semantics-preserving multi-stage programming.

### [3] [The Fourth-Root Complexity of Data Movement](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.30948)
*Chen Ding*

Main category: cs.CC

TL;DR: 本文基于抽象内存层次结构分析数据访问代价，发现对于常见应用，每次访问的代价随数据规模 $N$ 以 $N^{1/4}$ 的速率增长，并精确分析了缺失率遵循幂律与指数衰减时的常数因子差异，用于预测可扩展性而非具体性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统时间复杂度假设每次数据访问为 $O(1)$，但实际内存层次结构会导致访问代价随数据规模变化，需要更精确的建模来预测系统可扩展性。

Method: 基于抽象内存层次结构模型，分析数据访问代价与数据规模 $N$ 的关系，推导出代价按 $N^{1/4}$ 增长的规律，并比较了缺失率遵循幂律分布与指数衰减两种情况下的常数因子差异。

Result: 对于常见应用，数据访问代价随数据规模 $N$ 以 $N^{1/4}$ 的速率增长，且缺失率的分布形式（幂律 vs. 指数衰减）会导致常数因子上的差异。

Conclusion: 该分析不直接预测性能，但可用于预测系统的可扩展性，为理解内存层次结构对大规模数据处理的影响提供了理论依据。

Abstract: Time complexity typically assumes $O(1)$ cost per data access. This paper presents an analysis based on an abstract memory hierarchy. For a common class of applications, it shows that the data-access cost scales with the fourth root of data size, that is, as data size $N$ increases, the cost of each access increases at the rate of $N^\frac{1}{4}$.
  While the analysis does not predict performance, it predicts scalability. Specifically, the paper provides a precise analysis that shows the constant-factor difference between cases where the miss ratio follows a power law versus an exponential decay.

</details>

### [4] [A Multi-Dimensional, Per-Pass Empirical Study of the LLVM Optimization Pipeline](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.31238)
*Federico Bruzzone,Walter Cazzola*

Main category: cs.SE

TL;DR: 本文系统实证分析了LLVM -O3优化管线，通过113个累积前缀、84,750次测量，揭示了管线非单调性（6.6-9.7%的转换导致回退）、后部加载特性（中位数需要84.8%管线实现80%加速）、少数Pareto主导Pass贡献大部分收益、最终-O3配置在29/30核上被Pareto支配，以及IR指令数不可靠、运行时Pass同时节能30-60%、相位干扰理想化上限46.35%等关键发现。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 量化单个优化Pass的边际影响对于相位排序、Pass选择、优化设计及Pass/硬件交互分析至关重要，但LLVM中优化Pass间的交互、测量噪声和管线规模使得这一量化极具挑战。

Method: 将-O3管线分解为113个累积per-pass前缀，在30个PolyBench/C内核上执行84,750次测量，覆盖执行时间、编译时间、二进制大小、硬件计数器和RAPL能耗，并采用严格的噪声抑制措施。

Result: 管线非单调（6.6-9.7%转换出现回退）；后部加载（中位数需84.8%管线达80%加速）；少数Pareto主导Pass驱动多数收益；最终-O3在29/30核上对(大小,加速比)被Pareto支配；IR指令数不可靠预测运行时；运行时Pass同时节能30-60%；相位干扰无搜索理想化上限46.35%。

Conclusion: 这些发现可指导更明智的Pass剪枝、成本模型校准和自动调优。

Abstract: Quantifying the marginal impact of individual optimization passes underpins phase ordering, pass selection, optimization design, and analysis of pass/hardware interactions. In LLVM -- the standard backend for C/C++, Rust, and ML stacks via MLIR -- interactions among optimization passes, measurement noise, and pipeline scale make this difficult. We present a systematic, empirical study of the LLVM -O3 optimization pipeline. We decompose the pipeline into cumulative per-pass prefixes. We then measure execution time, compile time, binary size, hardware counters, and RAPL energy across 84,750 measurements covering 113 cumulative prefixes of the -O3 pipeline evaluated on 30 PolyBench/C kernels under rigorous noise mitigation. On these compute-bound affine kernels, the pipeline is non-monotone (6.6-9.7% of transitions regress) and strongly back-loaded (the median non-regressing kernel needs 84.8% of the pipeline for 80% of its speedup). Most gains are driven by a small Pareto-dominant core of passes, while the final -O3 configuration is Pareto-dominated on (size, speedup) for 29 of 30 kernels. We further show that IR instruction count is an unreliable predictor of runtime, that runtime-targeted passes are de facto energy-targeted (30-60% savings), and that the search-free idealized-additive upper bound on losses due to phase interference is 46.35%. These findings enable more informed pass pruning, cost-model calibration, and autotuning.

</details>
