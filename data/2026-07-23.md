### [1] [Generalized Constraint Projection: Four-Dimensional Type Inference for Dynamic Languages](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19693)
*Qunhui Zhang*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 提出了一种无需类型标注的推断框架GCP，通过分离四种证据来源、使用模板+投影机制，解决了动态语言类型推断中的冲突问题，并在Outline语言中实现，可恢复Python类型注解。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 动态语言类型推断面临函数参数的四种证据来源（内部赋值、显式声明、上下文需求、结构操作）相互冲突的问题，现有系统将它们合并为一个约束集导致虚假冲突或需要冗余标注。

Method: 提出GCP框架：将四种证据存储在单独的单调槽位上的稳定定义时模板中，每次调用在全新的投影会话中检查；使用OEM结构兼容性预序和开放双向委托协议；引入future this扩展支持接收器保留的流畅API子类型精化。

Result: 在有限高度类型预序的严格成功片段上证明了单调性、局部和全局收敛性（O(Nh_T)有效更新）、条件投影义务健全性、投影终止、多模块收敛性和公平单调迭代下的顺序无关性。对核心Outline0证明了确定性求值、类型保持、运行时接收器保留和投影-求值一致性。

Conclusion: GCP在Outline动态语言中实例化，并成功应用于无标注Python源码以恢复PEP 484类型注解，支持下游编译。

Abstract: Type inference for dynamically typed languages must reconcile four distinct sources of evidence for function parameters: internal assignments, explicit declarations, contextual requirements, and structural operations. Existing systems often merge these sources into one constraint set, causing spurious conflicts or requiring redundant annotations. We present Generalized Constraint Projection (GCP), a zero-annotation inference framework that stores the four sources in separate monotone slots on a stable definition-time template and checks each call in a fresh projection session. Ordinary calls verify concrete arguments and specialize return types without modifying the template, while currying produces residual projected functions. GCP uses Outline Equational Matching (OEM), a structural compatibility preorder with an open bidirectional delegation protocol, and future this, a receiver-preserving extension for subtype-refining fluent APIs. On the strict success fragment of a finite-height type preorder, we prove monotonicity, local and global convergence in $O(Nh_T)$ effective updates, conditional projection-obligation soundness, projection termination, multi-module convergence, and order independence under fair monotone iteration. For the pure, recursion-free core Outline0, we additionally prove big-step evaluation definedness, type preservation, runtime receiver retention, and projection-evaluation coherence. We instantiate GCP in the Outline dynamic language as a typed substrate for ontology worlds and apply it to unannotated Python source to recover PEP 484 annotations for downstream compilation.

### [2] [Towards Relating Ciao Assertions and LPTP Theorems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20249)
*Marco Pérez,Pedro López-García,Jose F. Morales,Manuel V. Hermenegildo,Fred Mesnard*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 本文研究了如何将Ciao Prolog系统中的断言规范转换为LPTP框架中的一阶逻辑公式，提出了一套系统翻译方案，并分析了不同断言类别的可编码性、近似策略以及由此带来的可靠性与完备性权衡。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Ciao Prolog的抽象解释验证与LPTP的演绎验证是两种互补的验证框架，但二者在规范表达上存在根本性差异。如何将Ciao的断言规范（基于抽象解释）翻译为LPTP的一阶逻辑公式（基于定理证明），是实现两者紧密集成的关键问题。

Method: 提出一套系统性的翻译方案，对Ciao断言按可逻辑编码性进行分类；针对不可翻译的情况设计近似策略和辅助构造；分析翻译结果在可靠性和完备性方面的权衡。

Result: 建立了Ciao断言到LPTP公式的部分对应关系；区分了可完全编码、需近似处理等不同断言类别；明确了不同翻译策略下的可靠性与完备性取舍。

Conclusion: 该翻译方案能够实现Ciao的断言检查与LPTP演绎验证的紧密集成，使两者互补能力得以充分发挥。

Abstract: Abstract interpretation-based verification is a central component of the Ciao Prolog system, enabling expressive specifications of properties of programs, predicates, and execution states. Independently, the LPTP (Logic Programming Theorem Proving) framework offers a first-order logical formalism for expressing and proving properties of predicates.  In this paper, we address a fundamental issue in relating these two frameworks: studying the translation of Ciao assertions into LPTP formulae and  identifying a partial correspondence between assertion-based and logic-based specifications. We introduce a systematic translation scheme, characterize assertion classes according to their logical encodability, and propose approximation strategies and auxiliary constructs for non-translatable cases, and finally analyze the resulting soundness and completeness trade-offs. We  argue that our proposal enables a tight integration of Ciao's assertion checking with LPTP-based deductive verification, thereby leveraging their complementary capabilities.

### [3] [Qoreo: Choreographic Programming for Quantum Distributed Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20391)
*Jennifer Paykin,Steven Baldasty,Joseph P. Near,Christian Skalka*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: Qoreo是一种用于分布式量子系统的编排式编程语言，允许将整个协议表达为单一全局程序（编排），而非多个独立进程的集合。它包含具有线性类型的局部量子语言、结合量子计算与经典/量子通信的编排语言，以及节点进程语言。该语言通过类型安全保证明确定义的量子操作，并通过端点投影自动生成无死锁的进程网络，其元理论在Rocq中完全机械化，并支持到NetQASM的提取以用于模拟和部署。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 直接以分布式进程编写量子协议繁琐且易出错，微小的不匹配可能导致死锁或静默错误的量子状态，因此需要一种更高级、更安全的抽象来简化分布式量子系统的编程。

Method: 提出Qoreo语言，包含三层：(1) 具有线性类型的局部量子语言，强制执行不可克隆原则；(2) 编排语言，将局部量子计算与actor间的经典和量子通信结合；(3) 用于单个网络节点的进程语言。通过端点投影(EPP)自动从编排生成独立进程网络，并在Rocq中完全机械化元理论。

Result: 证明了编排的类型安全性，保证良类型的程序实现定义良好的量子操作；证明了EPP相对编排语义的可靠性和完备性；作为推论，每个良类型的编排投影到无死锁的进程网络；提供了到NetQASM的提取管道用于模拟和部署。

Conclusion: Qoreo通过编排式编程范式，为分布式量子系统提供了一种类型安全、无死锁的高层抽象，显著降低了编程复杂度，其形式化元理论和实际部署管道的结合使该工作兼具理论深度和实用价值。

Abstract: Programming distributed quantum systems requires multiple actors to coordinate precise sequences of quantum operations, classical communication, and entanglement generation. Writing such protocols directly as distributed processes is tedious and error-prone, and subtle mismatches can cause deadlock or silently incorrect quantum states. We present Qoreo, a choreographic programming language for quantum distributed systems in which an entire protocol is expressed as single, global program (a choreography) rather than as a collection of independent actor processes. Qoreo includes a local quantum language with linear types that enforce the no-cloning principle; a choreographic language that combines local quantum computation with inter-actor classical and quantum communication; and a process language for individual network nodes. We prove type safety for choreographies, guaranteeing that well-typed programs implement well-defined quantum operations, and we define endpoint projection~(EPP), which automatically derives a network of independent processes from any choreography. We prove EPP sound and complete with respect to the choreographic semantics; as a corollary, every well-typed choreography projects to a deadlock-free process network. The metatheory of Qoreo is fully mechanized in Rocq, and we provide an extraction pipeline to NetQASM for simulation and deployment on quantum network hardware.

</details>

### [4] [Linearising Explicit Substitutions using Intersection Types](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20179)
*Ana Jorge Almeida,Sandra Alves,Mário Florido*

Main category: cs.LO

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种新的项展开方法，用于将带显式替换的lambda演算与Boudol的资源感知lambda演算（参数具有有限可用性）相关联。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 扩展项展开技术的应用范围，从传统的交集类型系统和线性项关联，推广至带显式替换的演算与资源感知演算之间的关联。

Method: 定义了一种针对显式替换演算的新型项展开方法，通过该方法建立lambda演算（含显式替换）与Boudol的资源感知lambda演算（支持参数有限可用性的多重性）之间的联系。

Result: 成功将项展开技术扩展至显式替换演算领域，建立了与资源感知lambda演算的对应关系。

Conclusion: 项展开方法具有更广泛的应用潜力，可推广至更多子结构类型系统和资源敏感性演算中。

Abstract: Term expansion was originally introduced in 2004 as a way to relate terms typed in an intersection type system with linear terms. Recently, new applications of term expansion include the relation of lambda-terms with terms typed in other substructural type systems, such as the relevant and the ordered type systems, and the use of quantitative types to relate the strongly normalising lambda-terms with weak linear terms that share the same normal form. Here we define a new term expansion for a calculus with explicit substitutions, using it to relate a lambda-calculus with explicit substitutions to Boudol's resource aware lambda-calculus with multiplicities, where function arguments have a possibly limited availability.

</details>
